About Us

Studies on birds have helped develop key concepts in biology including evolution, behaviour and ecology such as the definition of species, the process of speciation, instinct, learning, ecological niches, guilds, island biogeography, phylogeography, and conservation.

A research study of birds Ornithology is a branch of zoology that worries the “technical research as well as a subsequent understanding of birds with all that connects to them”. Several aspects of ornithology differ from associated self-controls, due partly to the high visibility and also the aesthetic appeal of birds.
